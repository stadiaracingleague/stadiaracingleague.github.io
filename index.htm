About

The Stadia Racing League is there to provide regular multiplayer events for every racing related game on Stadia (as long as a game finds the required playerbase).

By default the SRL offers serious, challenging and clean racing for all Stadia players around the world who are looking for a competitive racing experience. From time to time the SRL also offers fun events for the more casual type of players and games.

Rules Of Clean Racing (ROCR)

ALLOWED

  • smaller and often unavoidable touches between cars on straights
  • smaller bumps and slight leaning while overtaking in turns and breaking zones as long as it doesn't damage other players cars or forces it completely off the track, into walls, into barriers or into punishment zones
  • changing the line multiple times on a straight to break the slipstream as long as the following car is at least one car length behind
  • crossing lines and/or defensive blocking in turns as long as the following car is completely behind
  • cutting corners and using curbs as long as at least one part of the car is still within the track limits

PROHIBITED

  • destroying other players races and their clean racing experience on purpose
  • every kind of cheating, manipulating results and/or exploiting ingame mechanics* to gain an advantage
  • causing crashes and other race incidents on purpose
  • every kind of bumping and leaning on straights
  • bumps and leaning in turns and breaking zones that forces other players completely off the track, into walls, into barriers and into ingame punishment zones
  • defensive blocking on straights if the following car is just one car length or less behind
  • defensive blocking in breaking zones
  • exceeding track limits without slowing down noticeably (the track limits are exceeded when all parts of a car are beyond the white lines or other track limit markers)
  • driving back on track in an unsafe manner where you might collide with other cars
  • keep on racing with an obviously undriveable car
*In GRID
  • Instant reset after crashing somebody out
  • Self-DNF in hotlap to avoid DSQ (and to get a better GRID position)
  • Improve your starting position by leaving the session in hotlap and then rejoin it before the grid has loaded.
  • Decreasing laptime in wide off track areas with broken ingame time punishments
